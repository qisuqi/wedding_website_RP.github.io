Kang Foo Noodles

Mapo Tofu 🌶

Mapo Tofu 麻婆豆腐 is one of the most popular dishes from Sichuan cuisine. The tofu pieces are braised in a rich spicy, and savory sauce along with fresh garlic and green onions, with a small amount of ground pork to enhance the flavor. The dish is so appetising and it goes perfectly with steamed rice.

Braised Beef 🌶🌶

Sichuan Braised Beef 红烧牛肉 is a classic Sichuan dish known for its rich, savory, and slightly spicy flavor. The dish features tender chunks of beef slow-cooked in a soy sauce-based broth infused with aromatics like ginger, garlic, star anise, and Sichuan peppercorns, making it a comforting and flavorful staple in Chinese cuisine.

Tiger Skin Green Peppers (VE, VG)

Tiger Skin Green Peppers 虎皮青椒 is a flavorful Chinese dish named for the blistered, "tiger-striped" appearance of the peppers after being stir-fried. The green peppers are pan-seared until their skins char and blister, creating a smoky aroma. They are then seasoned with garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, and a touch of sugar for a perfect balance of savory, tangy, and slightly sweet flavors.
